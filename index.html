ISALs Portfolio Tracking Tool

Welcome to the ISALs Portfolio Tracking Tool project. This tool is designed to help NGOs empower rural women groups in Zimbabwe by tracking Internal Savings and Lending Schemes (ISALs).

This project is free for NGOs, as it aims to bring meaningful impact to rural livelihoods and help organizations attract further funding through transparent tracking.
